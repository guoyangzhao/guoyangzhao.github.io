Projects:

1. 24h Unmanned Patrol Vehicle System (2022.12-2024.08)

· Mainly responsible for the construction of the perception system based on multi-sensor.
· Multiple NeRFs online training and collaborative mapping in reconstruction task.
· Object detection of license plates and pedestrians, Multimodal image segmentation, LiDAR point cloud segmentation.
   

2. Soybean Seed Real-time Sorting System Based on Deep Learning (2019.05-2022.05, Project Leader)

· Construct a comprehensive dataset of full-surface defects in soybean seed.
· Optimize a lightweight model to realize accurate and real-time recognition in edge devices.
· Greatly improved the efficiency of selection in ellipsoid-like surface defects.
  

3. Research on field crop identification and navigation based on deep learning (2019.10-2022.01, main participant)

· Detect different weeds and seedlings of different leaf ages in the field environment.
· Transmit the position of weeds to the end effector, and spray pesticides on the weeds surface.
· Propose a row anchor selection classification method to help agricultural robots track crop rows.
